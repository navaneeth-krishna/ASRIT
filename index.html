about.

Astronomical society of RIT (ASRIT),an imprint of RIT ISTE chapter,funtioning under a set of constitutional bylaw is a platform for the amateur astronomers of Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology , Kottayam ,providing an ocean of opportunities and hopes to flee through the Galaxy of wonders.

Its the first step towards developing our own Newtonian Telescope during the time race.

vision.

To organize the astronomy enthusiasts and Popularize the role of engineers in space science.

mission.

To enhance the knowledge and interest of astronomy enthusiasts, to connect engineering disciplines with space technology and ultimately to be an active element in inscribing history by fostering our efforts from theory to practical aspects.

purpose.

  • Nurture scientific temparement among its members and the community as a whole.
  • Encourage its members to reach out to the scientific community and organisations in India or abroad.
  • Encourage contributions to the field of astronomy from engineering disciplines.
  • Organize events,viz. seminars,workshops, hands-on sessions, quizzes,etc. to the field of astronomy and engineering.
